Welcome to the mobile motion capture revolution

mocopi is an ideal motion capture system for those just getting started in the world of full-body tracking. With just six lightweight, easy-to-use sensors, it's simpler than ever for you to create 3D content you'll love.

 
 

Simply put on the sensors, sync to the free app, and you're ready to go:

• No studio setup needed-you can use mocopi anywhere

• Create avatar videos vou can post on social media

• Send your avatar to video editing software for more customization

• Choose from a number of different backgrounds

• Place your avatar in the real world using the AR background feature

• Easily set up full body movement in VRChat

• Use voice recording that syncs with your avatar's lip movement
